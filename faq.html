Take precautions and help us in flattening the curve

Together we can fight Covid 19

Wear Mask

Place the mask over your nose, mouth and chin and ensure there are no gaps on either side of the mask, adjust to fit.

Wash Hands

Wash your hands with soap and water regularly . If water is available use sanitizer with at least 60% alcohol

Sneeze and Cough

Sneeze and cough on a tissue and dispose it properly in the dustbin

Stay Home

Stay Home and keep safe distance from other people.

Practice Food Safety

Use different chopping boards and knives for food.Cook the food properly
